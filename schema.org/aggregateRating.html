aggregateRating

Thing > Property > aggregateRating

The overall rating, based on a collection of reviews or ratings, of the item.
  • Canonical URL: http://schema.org/aggregateRating
  • Leave public feedback on this term 💬
  • Check for open issues.
Values expected to be one of these types
AggregateRating
Used on these types
Brand
CreativeWork
Event
Offer
Organization
Place
Product
Service
